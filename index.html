📋 Game Rules

You are a librarian, your job is to collect books from the messy floor and put the books in the right section in the right order.
🚶🏻‍➡️ - To Move on the Floor or the Bookshelves, For Mobile Device use you finger. For PC use Mouse Scroll Wheel (Vertical movement) and Shift + Mouse Scroll Wheel (Horizontal movement).
🫳 - To pick a book form the floor, click on the book. The book will be added to the top of your hand. You can only hold a limited number of books in your hand.
🫸 - To place a book on the shelf, click on the slot. The book will be placed in the slot.
🔴 Warning : If you put a book in the wrong section or wrong slot, an error glow indicator will occur.
🫴 - To retract a book from the shelf back to your hand, click on the book in the shelf. The book will be removed from the shelf and added to the top of your hand.
▲ Move Up : To move the top book in your hand to the bottom of your hand, click the "Move Up" button.
▼ Move Down : To move the bottom book in your hand to the top of your hand, click the "Move Down" button.
🗑️ Discard : To remove a book from your hand, click the "Discard Top" button. The top book will be removed and placed back on the floor at a random location.
💰 Coins : Competing a row will give you coins. You also have abilities.

The abilities are:
1. 📚 Quick Sort: Automatically categorizes and sort the books in your hand.
2. 🔍 Locate Book: Highlights potential matches directly on the floor as well as shelfs.
3. 🗄️ Locate Shelf: Instantly flashes destination Section for easy filing.
4. 🧲 Magic Pool: Magnetically pulls corresponding volume sequences directly to your hand.
- You can upgrade your abilities -.
